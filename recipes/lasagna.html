Lasagna

A delicious, oven baked lasagna

This dish is my take on a family classic, the homemade lasagna! Just in time for winter's chill; this recipe is the perfect blend of meaty red sauce, creamy ricotta and stretchy mozzarella, and succulent, al dente lasagna noodles!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb dry lasagna pasta

For the meat sauce:

  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 2 carrots, finely diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 28 oz petite diced tomatoes
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp fresh basil, chiffonade
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp fresh oregano, finely chopped

For the cheese layer:

  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 lb fresh, sliced mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • chopped parsley, as desired, for color

Steps:

Preparing the layers:

  1. fill a large pot with water and generously sprinkle with salt. set over medium high heat
  2. while water heats, add olive oil to a deep skillet over medium high heat
  3. add onion and carrots. cook until tender (approx. 5 mins)
  4. add garlic, and cook for another 30 seconds, until aromatic
  5. add ground beef, and begin to break apart; cooking until there is no more pink
  6. while beef cooks, mix the ricotta, egg, and grated parmesan in a mixing bowl
  7. once there is no more pink in the beef, drain the grease
  8. return beef and vegetables to the pan, add diced and crushed tomatoes, bring mixture to a boil (add water to thin out if mixture becomes too thick), then simmer for at least 30 minutes
  9. once pot of water is boiling, add dry pasta and prepare according to box instructions
  10. strain noodles, and set aside
  11. once 30 minutes have passed, add chopped basil, parsley, and oregano to meat sauce, cook for 1 minute longer or until aromatic

Assembly:

  1. spread a thin layer of sauce over the bottom of the pan (this will prevent sticking)
  2. begin with a layer of noodles
  3. now, add a layer of sauce
  4. next, a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture
  5. follow this with a layer of mozzarella
  6. another layer of sauce
  7. finally, repeat these steps until you reach the top of the pan, preferrably with a layer of the ricotta mixture
  8. add one last layer of the fresh mozzarella

Baking:

  1. preheat oven to 425
  2. cover pan with aluminum foil
  3. bake in oven for 30 minutes, covered
  4. after 30 minutes, remove foil, and place back in oven
  5. bake for an additional 10 to 15 minutes, or until cheese is golden brown
