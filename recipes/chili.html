Beef Chili

A bowl of delightful, warm beef chili

Packed with nutrients and warmth is this classic Chili recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Chili powder
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp garlic power
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp regular or smoked paprika
  • 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 2 poblano peppers, finely diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 14.5 oz cans of diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 24 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2-4 cups beef broth
  • 1/4 cup worcestershire sauce

Steps:

  1. mix all spices together and set aside
  2. set a heavy dutch oven over medium high heat
  3. add oil once hot, add diced onion and poblanos, cooking until soft (about 5 mins)
  4. once softened, add minced garlic and cook until aromatic (about 1 min)
  5. add ground beef, break up and create a single layer, and cook until there is no more pink
  6. once ground beef is cooked, strain fat out and set meat/veggie mixture to the side
  7. add tomato paste to hot pan, and cook until dark, burnt orange color (about 3 mins)
  8. add meat/veggie mixture back to the pan, stir until combined with tomato paste
  9. add cans of diced tomatoes, use juice to deglaze pan
  10. add crushed tomatoes, stir until combined, heat until bubbling
  11. add beef broth (CAUTION: only mix enough to thin out, you may not need all 4 cups)
  12. turn heat down to low, cover dutch oven with lid, and allow chili to simmer at least 1 hour and up to 4 hours to let flavors fully meld together
  13. once finished cooking, add worcestershire sauce (add more or less as desired) and stir to combine
  14. remove from heat and serve with sour cream, green onion, crackers, or any other toppings you wish!
